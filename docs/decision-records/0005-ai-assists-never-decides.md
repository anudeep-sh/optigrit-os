# ADR-0005

## Title

AI Assists. Teachers Decide.

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Artificial Intelligence is increasingly capable of generating educational content.

However, educational responsibility cannot be delegated to AI.

Teachers understand context that models cannot fully capture.

---

## Decision

AI recommendations remain recommendations.

Teachers remain decision makers.

Every AI-generated artifact should be reviewable before publication.

---

## Allowed AI Actions

Generate lesson plans.

Draft assessments.

Summarize discussions.

Recommend resources.

Predict struggling students.

Generate analytics.

Prepare lecture notes.

---

## Disallowed AI Actions

Publish grades autonomously.

Modify curriculum autonomously.

Replace institutional policy.

Communicate disciplinary actions.

Make irreversible academic decisions.

---

## Consequences

Higher trust.

Lower automation rate.

Improved educational accountability.

Compliance with institutional governance.

---

## Long-Term Vision

The best AI is invisible.

Teachers should feel more capable—not replaced.
