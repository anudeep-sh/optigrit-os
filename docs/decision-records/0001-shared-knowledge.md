# ADR-0001

## Title

Knowledge Belongs to Institutions

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

Traditional educational systems encourage teachers to recreate educational material every semester.

This creates duplication, inconsistent quality and loss of institutional knowledge.

---

## Decision

Courses, Learning Assets and educational material belong to institutions.

Teachers contribute improvements.

Institutions retain ownership.

---

## Consequences

Positive

Knowledge improves continuously.

Faculty collaboration.

Reduced duplication.

Institutional memory.

Negative

Approval workflow required.

Versioning required.

Contribution tracking required.

Conflict resolution required.

---

## Alternatives Considered

Faculty-owned courses.

Rejected.

Reason:

Knowledge should survive faculty turnover.
