# Product Language

Every person working on OptiGrit should use consistent terminology.

The product language defines what words mean inside OptiGrit.

---

Institution

A legal educational entity.

---

Program

A structured educational offering.

Examples

B.Tech

MBA

BCA

---

Batch

A cohort admitted in a particular academic year.

---

Semester

A time-bound academic period.

---

Subject

A curriculum definition.

Independent of learning material.

---

Course

A reusable implementation of learning.

Contains learning assets.

Can support multiple subjects.

---

Learning Asset

The smallest reusable learning component.

Examples

Video

Document

Assignment

Contest

Reading

AI Session

External Resource

---

Teaching Schedule

Defines when teaching happens.

Independent of attendance.

---

Lecture

A single teaching session.

Occurs within a teaching schedule.

May be online or offline.

---

Assessment

A structured evaluation.

May contain multiple question types.

---

Progress

A learner's personal educational journey.

Never belongs to a course.

Always belongs to a learner.

---

Analytics

Information that helps educators improve learning.

Analytics should always recommend action.

Never simply display charts.
