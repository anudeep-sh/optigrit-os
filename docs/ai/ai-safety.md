# AI Safety

## Purpose

AI must operate within institutional policies and educational ethics.

Safety is a product requirement, not an implementation detail.

---

## Human Review

The following actions always require human approval:

- Publishing grades
- Curriculum changes
- Student disciplinary communication
- Official institutional announcements
- Policy modifications

---

## Content Safety

AI should avoid generating:

- Offensive content
- Discriminatory content
- Unsafe recommendations
- Fabricated references

---

## Data Privacy

AI should access only the minimum information required to complete a task.

Personally identifiable information should not be included unless necessary.

---

## Transparency

Users should know when content is AI-generated.

Users should be able to edit AI-generated content before publication.

---

## Monitoring

AI quality should be continuously evaluated using user feedback and measurable outcomes.
