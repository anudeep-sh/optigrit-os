# AI Capabilities

## Purpose

This document defines the capabilities AI may provide across the OptiGrit platform.

AI capabilities are grouped by business domain rather than by underlying model or technology.

---

# Knowledge

## Generate Course Outline

Create a structured course outline from a subject, syllabus or learning objectives.

---

## Improve Existing Course

Suggest improvements based on learner engagement, assessment performance and curriculum changes.

---

## Generate Learning Assets

Create quizzes, summaries, notes, flashcards, presentations or practice exercises from existing content.

---

## Recommend Resources

Recommend books, videos, articles and external references relevant to the current topic.

---

# Planning

## Lesson Planning

Generate lesson plans aligned with academic calendars and learning outcomes.

---

## Teaching Pace

Recommend whether upcoming lectures should accelerate, slow down or include revision.

---

## Workload Planning

Identify overloaded teaching schedules and suggest redistribution.

---

# Teaching

## Lecture Preparation

Prepare lecture notes, examples and discussion points.

---

## Classroom Assistant

Answer faculty questions during lecture preparation.

Generate explanations at different difficulty levels.

---

## Lecture Summary

Automatically summarize completed lectures for future reference.

---

# Learning

## Personalized Learning

Recommend learning paths based on learner performance.

---

## Doubt Resolution

Provide contextual explanations using approved institutional content.

---

## Revision Planning

Generate revision schedules before assessments.

---

# Assessment

## Question Generation

Generate questions mapped to learning outcomes and difficulty levels.

---

## Rubric Generation

Create evaluation rubrics for descriptive assessments.

---

## Feedback Assistance

Generate draft feedback for faculty review.

---

# Analytics

## Early Risk Detection

Identify learners requiring intervention.

---

## Trend Analysis

Identify recurring learning gaps across batches or semesters.

---

## Institutional Insights

Recommend curriculum improvements using historical data.

---

# Communication

## Announcement Drafting

Generate announcements from structured inputs.

---

## Discussion Summaries

Summarize long discussions into key decisions and unresolved questions.

---

## Translation

Translate educational content while preserving meaning.

---

# Principles

AI capabilities remain optional.

Every capability should improve productivity without reducing human oversight.
