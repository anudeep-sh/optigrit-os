# AI Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence should amplify educators.

Not replace them.

---

## Four Levels Of Intelligence

Level 1

Retrieve

Search.

Recommend.

Summarize.

---

Level 2

Assist

Generate.

Organize.

Draft.

Prepare.

---

Level 3

Automate

Remind.

Schedule.

Classify.

Categorize.

---

Level 4

Collaborate

Suggest interventions.

Identify struggling learners.

Recommend curriculum improvements.

Generate institutional insights.

---

## Human Authority

Every educational decision belongs to humans.

AI contributes information.

Humans contribute judgment.

This distinction should never disappear.

---

## Trust

Users should know:

What AI generated.

Why.

Using which information.

With what confidence.

Trust determines adoption.

Not intelligence.
