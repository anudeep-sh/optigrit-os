# Privacy

## Purpose

Privacy ensures that personal data is collected, processed and retained responsibly.

Educational institutions own their data.

OptiGrit acts as a trusted platform for managing it.

---

## Principles

Collect only the data required to provide the service.

Users should understand what data is collected and why.

Personal data should not be retained longer than necessary.

---

## Data Ownership

Institutions own institutional data.

Users own personal preferences and profile information where applicable.

OptiGrit does not claim ownership over educational content created by institutions.

---

## AI

AI should use the minimum information required.

Institution-specific information should remain isolated.

AI-generated outputs should respect institutional privacy policies.

---

## Data Export

Institutions should be able to export their data.

Exports should use open formats whenever practical.

---

## Data Deletion

Deletion requests should follow institutional policy and applicable regulations.

Historical academic records may require retention according to institutional requirements.
