# Announcement

## Definition

An Announcement communicates important information to one or more audiences.

Announcements are one-way communications.

They are intended to inform rather than facilitate discussion.

---

## Owns

- Title
- Content
- Audience
- Publication Time
- Expiry Time
- Attachments

---

## Relationships

Institution

Course

Lecture

Assessment

Announcements may belong to any of these contexts.

---

## Invariants

Announcements preserve publication history.

Announcements remain referenceable.

Edits are versioned.
