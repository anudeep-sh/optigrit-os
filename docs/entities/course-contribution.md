# Course Contribution

## Definition

A Course Contribution records a proposed or accepted improvement made to a Course by a Faculty member or reviewer.

It supports collaborative knowledge evolution while preserving institutional ownership.

---

## Owns

- Contributor
- Contribution Type
- Description
- Review Status
- Reviewer
- Decision Date

---

## Contribution Types

- New Content
- Content Update
- Resource Addition
- Assessment Update
- Structural Change

---

## Lifecycle

Submitted

↓

Under Review

↓

Approved

↓

Merged

or

↓

Rejected

---

## Invariants

Every Contribution targets one Course.

Rejected Contributions remain part of the audit history.

Approved Contributions become part of a future Course Version.
