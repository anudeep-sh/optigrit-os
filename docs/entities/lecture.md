# Lecture

## Definition

A Lecture represents a single teaching event.

Unlike Courses, which are reusable,

Lectures happen once.

They exist within time.

---

## Contains

Teaching context.

Attendance.

Discussion.

Teaching Notes.

Announcements.

Resources shared during class.

---

## Relationship

Teaching Schedule

↓

Lecture

↓

Attendance

↓

Student Participation

---

## Why Separate Lecture From Teaching Schedule?

Teaching Schedule represents intent.

Lecture represents execution.

Schedules may change.

Lectures record reality.

---

## Invariants

Every Lecture has a planned start time.

A Lecture may deviate from its plan.

Attendance belongs to the Lecture.

Teaching Notes belong to the Lecture.
