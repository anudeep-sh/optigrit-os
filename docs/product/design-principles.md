# Product Design Principles

These principles guide every feature developed for OptiGrit.

---

## Teacher First

Reducing teacher effort creates better learning experiences.

Every feature should save educator time.

---

## Learning Before Administration

Administrative workflows should never dominate the learning experience.

---

## One Source of Truth

Business information should exist only once.

Duplication creates inconsistency.

---

## Continuous Improvement

The platform should become more valuable with every semester.

Historical data exists to improve future teaching.

---

## Reusable Knowledge

Educational content should be reusable across Courses, batches and academic years.

---

## Explainable Analytics

Analytics should answer:

"What happened?"

"Why did it happen?"

"What should happen next?"

---

## AI is Assistive

AI drafts.

AI recommends.

AI summarizes.

Humans decide.

---

## Simplicity Over Configuration

Prefer sensible defaults.

Configuration should solve real institutional differences, not product shortcomings.
