# Core Workflows

## Purpose

Workflows describe how business processes move through the platform.

Unlike User Journeys, which are persona-centric, Workflows are business-centric and may involve multiple personas.

## Workflow Principles

Every workflow should:

- Have a clearly defined start.
- Have a measurable completion.
- Emit business events.
- Support auditability.
- Be resumable after interruptions.
