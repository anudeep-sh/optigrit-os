# AI Philosophy

AI is embedded throughout OptiGrit.

It is never the destination.

---

# AI Should Reduce Work

AI should automate repetitive activities such as:

- Summarization
- Question Generation
- Lesson Planning
- Content Organization
- Draft Feedback

---

# AI Should Increase Quality

AI should improve consistency.

Identify gaps.

Recommend improvements.

Surface insights.

---

# AI Should Never Replace Educators

Academic judgment belongs to humans.

AI supports decisions.

It does not own them.

---

# AI Should Learn Context

Institutional context.

Course context.

Historical teaching.

Learning Outcomes.

Academic policies.

Context improves usefulness.

---

# Long-Term Vision

AI should become a trusted academic assistant that understands the institution while remaining transparent, controllable and accountable.
