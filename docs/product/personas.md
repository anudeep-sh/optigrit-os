# Personas

## Purpose

Personas represent the primary users of OptiGrit.

Every feature should identify its primary persona before implementation.

---

## Faculty

Goals

- Deliver effective teaching.
- Reduce administrative workload.
- Track learner progress.

Primary Activities

- Planning
- Teaching
- Evaluation
- Mentoring

---

## Learner

Goals

- Learn effectively.
- Complete academic requirements.
- Improve competencies.

Primary Activities

- Learning
- Practice
- Assessment
- Discussion

---

## Institution Administrator

Goals

- Manage institutional operations.
- Monitor academic health.
- Improve educational quality.

Primary Activities

- Administration
- Planning
- Reporting
- Governance

---

## Department Head

Goals

- Support faculty.
- Monitor department performance.
- Improve curriculum.

Primary Activities

- Faculty Management
- Academic Review
- Resource Planning

---

## Parent (Optional)

Goals

- Understand learner progress.
- Receive important updates.

Primary Activities

- Progress Review
- Communication
