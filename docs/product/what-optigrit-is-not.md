# What OptiGrit Is Not

Understanding what OptiGrit is not is as important as understanding what it is.

---

## Not Just Another LMS

Learning Management Systems primarily manage content.

OptiGrit manages the entire educational lifecycle.

---

## Not Just Video Hosting

Videos are one form of Learning Asset.

Education is much broader than recorded lectures.

---

## Not Just Assessments

Assessments measure learning.

They are not the learning process itself.

---

## Not Just Analytics

Analytics without action have little value.

OptiGrit connects analytics directly to future teaching improvements.

---

## Not an AI Product

AI is a capability throughout the platform.

It is not the platform itself.

---

## Not a Collection of Features

Courses, Planning, Teaching, Learning, Assessment, Analytics and AI are parts of one continuous educational system.

The whole is more valuable than the sum of its individual features.
