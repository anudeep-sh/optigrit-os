# Course Lifecycle

## Purpose

Courses are living academic assets.

They continuously evolve based on educational outcomes.

---

# Stage 1

Design

Faculty create learning structure.

Learning Outcomes are defined.

Resources are organized.

---

# Stage 2

Review

Academic review ensures quality.

Contributors collaborate.

Feedback is incorporated.

---

# Stage 3

Publication

The Course becomes available for Teaching.

Published versions become historical references.

---

# Stage 4

Delivery

Teaching Schedules use the Course.

Lectures are conducted.

Learners engage.

Assessments occur.

---

# Stage 5

Observation

Analytics measure:

- Engagement
- Completion
- Assessment Results
- Learning Gaps

---

# Stage 6

Improvement

Faculty improve:

- Content
- Activities
- Assessments
- Learning Resources

A new Course Version is produced.

The previous version remains available for historical reference.
