# Notification Strategy

## Purpose

Notifications should increase awareness without creating distraction.

Every notification competes for the user's attention.

Attention is a limited resource.

---

## Notification Categories

### Critical

Require immediate attention.

Examples

- Assessment submission failure.
- Lecture cancelled.
- Institution-wide emergency.

---

### Action Required

Require user action.

Examples

- Pending evaluation.
- Assignment due.
- Approval request.

---

### Informational

Provide awareness.

Examples

- New resource published.
- Course updated.
- Discussion reply.

---

### Digest

Summarize activity.

Examples

Daily summary.

Weekly summary.

Monthly report.

---

## Delivery Channels

- In-App
- Email
- Push Notification
- SMS
- WhatsApp (institution configured)

---

## Principles

Notifications should be:

Relevant.

Timely.

Actionable.

Context-aware.

Never duplicated across channels unless explicitly configured.

---

## Quiet Hours

Institutions may define quiet hours during which non-critical notifications are deferred.
