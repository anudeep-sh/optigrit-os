# Glossary

## Purpose

This glossary defines the canonical meaning of terms used throughout OptiGrit.

Every feature, document and discussion should use these definitions consistently.

---

## Institution

An educational organization using OptiGrit.

---

## Subject

A curriculum-defined area of study describing *what* should be taught.

---

## Course

The instructional implementation of a Subject describing *how* it is taught.

---

## Section

A logical grouping of learning content within a Course.

---

## Learning Asset

Reusable educational content such as videos, documents, labs, quizzes or external resources.

---

## Teaching Schedule

A semester or batch specific teaching plan created from a Course.

---

## Lecture

One scheduled teaching session within a Teaching Schedule.

---

## Learning Session

A learner's interaction with educational content.

---

## Assessment

A structured evaluation of learning.

---

## Question Bank

A reusable repository of assessment questions.

---

## Submission

A learner's response to an Assessment.

---

## Result

The published outcome of an Assessment.

---

## Progress

A computed representation of learning completion.

---

## Competency

A measurable capability acquired through learning.

---

## Learning Outcome

A statement describing what learners should know or be able to do.

---

## Faculty

A person responsible for planning, teaching or evaluating learners.

---

## Learner

A person participating in learning activities.

---

## Analytics

Insights generated from educational activities to improve future learning.

---

## Recommendation

An actionable suggestion generated through Analytics or AI.

---

## AI

An assistant that accelerates educational workflows without replacing human judgment.
