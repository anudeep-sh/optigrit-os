# Analytics Domain

## Purpose

Analytics transforms educational data into actionable insights.

Analytics should answer questions.

Not merely display numbers.

---

# Philosophy

Every chart should support a decision.

If no action can be taken,

the visualization probably shouldn't exist.

---

# Responsibilities

Measure learning.

Measure teaching.

Measure engagement.

Identify risks.

Recommend interventions.

Evaluate institutional performance.

---

# Analytics Layers

Operational Analytics

Daily teaching.

Strategic Analytics

Semester trends.

Predictive Analytics

Future outcomes.

Prescriptive Analytics

Recommended actions.

---

# AI Integration

Detect struggling students.

Predict course completion.

Recommend interventions.

Suggest teaching improvements.

Highlight curriculum gaps.

---

# Success

Analytics succeeds when teachers say:

"I know what to do next."

Not:

"I understand this chart."
