# Planning Domain

## Purpose

Planning transforms reusable knowledge into a teaching plan.

Knowledge answers:

"What can be taught?"

Planning answers:

"What will be taught, to whom, and when?"

---

# Core Concepts

Academic Calendar

Teaching Schedule

Lesson Plan

Lecture Preparation

---

# Responsibilities

Create teaching schedules.

Allocate faculty.

Prepare lesson plans.

Sequence learning.

Estimate teaching workload.

Recommend pacing.

Support timetable generation.

---

# Relationship With Knowledge

Planning references Courses.

Planning never owns Courses.

This distinction allows the same Course to be reused across multiple teaching plans.

---

# Relationship With Teaching

Planning creates intent.

Teaching records execution.

These are intentionally different domains.

A Lecture exists because a Teaching Schedule planned it.

A Lecture is not the Teaching Schedule itself.
