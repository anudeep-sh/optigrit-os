# AI Domain

## Purpose

Artificial Intelligence is not a standalone feature.

It is a capability that enhances every other domain.

AI exists to reduce cognitive and operational workload.

---

# Guiding Principle

AI replaces repetitive software interactions.

AI never replaces educational responsibility.

Teachers remain accountable.

AI remains assistive.

---

# AI Responsibilities

Generate.

Recommend.

Summarize.

Predict.

Organize.

Explain.

Automate.

---

# Domain Integration

Knowledge

Generate learning material.

Summarize resources.

Recommend references.

Planning

Generate lesson plans.

Predict pacing.

Recommend schedules.

Teaching

Prepare lectures.

Capture notes.

Summarize discussions.

Assessment

Generate questions.

Assist evaluation.

Create rubrics.

Analytics

Predict outcomes.

Recommend interventions.

Explain trends.

---

# Design Principles

Every AI action should be:

Transparent.

Reviewable.

Editable.

Explainable.

Reversible whenever possible.

---

# Long-Term Vision

The ultimate goal is not to build an AI-powered LMS.

The goal is to build a platform where AI quietly removes operational friction, allowing educators to focus on human interaction, creativity, and mentorship.
