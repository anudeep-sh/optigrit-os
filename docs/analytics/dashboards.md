# Dashboards

## Purpose

Dashboards provide decision support.

A dashboard is not a reporting screen.

It should answer:

"What needs my attention?"

---

## Faculty Dashboard

Focus on today.

Include:

- Upcoming Lectures
- Pending Attendance
- Pending Evaluations
- Student Alerts
- AI Suggestions

---

## Learner Dashboard

Focus on learning.

Include:

- Continue Learning
- Pending Tasks
- Upcoming Assessments
- Progress Summary
- Recommendations

---

## Administrator Dashboard

Focus on operations.

Include:

- Active Programs
- Faculty Workload
- Institutional Health
- Completion Trends
- Risk Indicators

---

## Principles

Every widget must answer one question.

Widgets should support drill-down.

Information density should remain low.

Actions should be available directly from the dashboard.
