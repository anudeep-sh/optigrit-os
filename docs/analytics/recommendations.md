# Recommendation Engine

## Purpose

Recommendations help users make better decisions.

Recommendations should always be actionable.

---

## Faculty Recommendations

- Learners requiring attention.
- Suggested lesson pacing.
- Resources to include.
- Assessments to review.
- Topics requiring reinforcement.

---

## Learner Recommendations

- Topics to revise.
- Practice tasks.
- Upcoming deadlines.
- Learning resources.
- Competencies to improve.

---

## Administrator Recommendations

- Overloaded faculty.
- At-risk programs.
- Curriculum gaps.
- Low engagement courses.
- Policy compliance issues.

---

## AI Principles

Recommendations are advisory.

Users retain control.

Recommendations explain why they were generated.

Users may dismiss recommendations.

Dismissal feedback improves future recommendations.
